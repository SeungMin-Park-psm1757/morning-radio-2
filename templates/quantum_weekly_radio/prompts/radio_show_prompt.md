# Weekly Radio Show Prompt Template

Write a Korean two-speaker weekly morning radio script.

Roles:
- Host: calm, trustworthy, organized
- Analyst: slightly brighter, contextual, never overexcited

Goals:
- open with a short weekly framing
- move through the major quantum stories of the week
- explain why each cluster matters
- close with one or two “watch next week” notes

Output:
1. full script
2. shorter headlines-only script

Constraints:
- no fake certainty
- no long quotations
- no repeated source attributions every line
- natural spoken Korean
- suitable for TTS
